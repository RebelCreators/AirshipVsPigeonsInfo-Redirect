Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Airship vs Pigeons ("the App") is a mobile arcade game. This policy explains what data is collected when you play and how it is used.

Data We Collect Directly

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information. The App stores game progress (scores, awards, settings) locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently.

Google AdMob
The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) and usage data to serve personalised or contextual ads. If you have not granted tracking permission, only contextual ads are shown. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Firebase Analytics
The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised usage data (session length, feature usage, crash reports). This data is not linked to your identity. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Apple Game Center
Leaderboard and achievement data is handled by Apple Game Center under your Apple ID. We do not have access to your Apple ID or personal details. See apple.com/legal/privacy.

Apple In-App Purchases
Purchases (e.g. Remove Ads) are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App requests permission to track your activity across other apps and websites using your device's advertising identifier. You may allow or deny this request. Denying tracking does not affect gameplay.

You can change your preference at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store personal data on our own servers. To remove locally stored game data, delete the App from your device. For data held by third-party services, refer to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
