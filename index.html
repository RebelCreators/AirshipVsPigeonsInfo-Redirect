Simple controls.
Brutal
challenge.

Two buttons. Your airship rises and falls. The sky fills with pigeons. Keep flying as long as you can and rack up coins before something brings you down.

  1. Rise — hold the left flame button to climb higher.
  2. Descend — hold the right flame button to drop lower.
  3. Boost — tap the centre button the moment a power-up icon appears.
  4. Combo — knock pigeons into planes and bricks to chain coins together.
